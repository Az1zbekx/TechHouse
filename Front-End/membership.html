Frequently Asked Questions

How do loyalty points work?
Earn points on every purchase and redeem them for discounts on future orders. Bronze members earn 1 point per $1, Silver 2 points, and Gold 3 points.
Can I upgrade my membership?
Yes! You can upgrade at any time. Your new benefits will be available immediately, and we'll prorate the cost.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with no cancellation fees.
How does free delivery work?
Gold members get free standard delivery on all orders. Express delivery is available at a discounted rate.
