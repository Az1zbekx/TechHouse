About Tech House

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Tech House has been dedicated to bringing the latest and most innovative home appliances to families across Uzbekistan. We believe that modern technology should make life easier, more comfortable, and more enjoyable.

What started as a small showroom in Tashkent has grown into a trusted brand with multiple locations and thousands of satisfied customers. Our commitment to quality, customer service, and innovation remains unwavering.
